Streaming quotes for a group of instruments

This is a demo on showing price updates. The app is made the primary one (entitled for realtime quotes, if customer has a subscription) and this state is monitored.
On SIM, only prices for FxSpot are available, unless you connect your SIM account with your Live account.
When testing on live, you might need to enable OpenApi DataAccess in your account in order to get prices.
More info about prices can be found in the learn section.
